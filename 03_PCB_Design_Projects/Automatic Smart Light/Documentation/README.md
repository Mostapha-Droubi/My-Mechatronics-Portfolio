## 💡 Circuit Theory
* **Sensor Logic**: The system uses a photoresistor (LDR) to detect ambient light. In darkness, the LDR resistance is high, increasing the voltage at the transistor base to switch the relay ON[cite: 1].
* **Switching Logic**: A 2N2222A NPN transistor acts as an electronic switch, allowing a low-power control signal to drive the high-current relay coil[cite: 1].
* **Safety**: A 1N4007 flyback diode is installed to protect the transistor from back EMF generated by the relay coil[cite: 1].
